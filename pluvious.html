Pluvious Group
Campaign Finance Intern
September 2016 - December 2016

While an intern at Pluvious Group in 2016, I fundraised for Kathryn Barger in her bid to become a Los Angeles County Supervisor. Over three months of performing donor research and proactively soliciting donations over the phone and at fundraiser events, I individually raised over $6000 in event attendee contributions to be put towards the campaign. Kathryn won her race and now serves as Supervisor of Los Angeles County's 5th District.
